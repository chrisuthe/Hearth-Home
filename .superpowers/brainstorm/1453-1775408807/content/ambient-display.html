Ambient Behaviors

Photo Transitions
Ken Burns (slow zoom + pan) with crossfade between photos. New photo every ~15 seconds.
Night Mode
After sunset: dim to minimum brightness, show only clock on dark background. No photos (prevents room glow).
Context Overlays
Now-playing pill appears when music is active. Fades in/out smoothly. Weather updates periodically via HA.
Recommendation: Start with A (Nest Hub Style). The contextual overlays are what make it feel "smart" vs a basic photo frame. The overlays use semi-transparent backgrounds so the photo still dominates. On an AMOLED with 24000:1 contrast, the dark gradients will look incredible — the overlays will feel like they float on the image.
