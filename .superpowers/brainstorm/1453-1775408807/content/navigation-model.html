Navigation Rules

Screen Order (horizontal swipe)
Media ← Home → Controls → Cameras → Settings
Home is the center. PageView with snapping physics.
Ambient ↔ Active
Tap ambient → Home. Idle 2min → fade to Ambient.
Ambient is a layer above, not part of the PageView.
Event Interrupts
Overlay on top of everything. Priority: safety > doorbell > info.
Doorbell auto-shows camera. Safety alerts are persistent.
Swipe Physics
Snappy, slightly dampened. Not floaty, not rigid.
Match Nest Hub feel: quick flick to next, slow drag snaps back.
